There are so many places I want to go to

Grand Tetons

The Grand Tetons are a mountain range in northwestern Wyoming. They are a paradise for hiking, skiing, rock climbing, and animal viewing.

Mount Katahdin

Katahdin is the tallest mountain in Maine. It is famous as the northern terminus of The Appalachian Trail, a hiking path that stretches almost 2200 miles all the way from Georgia.

Sierra Nevada Mountains

The Sierra Nevada Mountains are the tallest mountains in the lower 48. They stretch through California, encompassing national parks like Sequoia, Kings Canyon, and Yosemite. They are famous for impressive granite features and high mountain passes.
